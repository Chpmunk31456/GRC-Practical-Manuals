## Important Notice

This material is provided for education and general operational resilience guidance.

Incident response, business continuity, and disaster recovery requirements vary according to an organization’s industry, location, systems, critical services, contractual obligations, and applicable laws and regulations.

Organizations should adapt the guidance to their actual risks, business priorities, recovery capabilities, and technical environment.
