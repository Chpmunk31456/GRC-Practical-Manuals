## Important Notice

This material is provided for education and general compliance guidance.

It is not legal, medical, regulatory, or audit advice. HIPAA obligations depend on the organization’s role, systems, contracts, data, operations, and applicable federal and state requirements.

Organizations should verify requirements through current official guidance from the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services and qualified legal, privacy, security, or compliance professionals.
